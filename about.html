Who We Are

The Mathematics, Engineering, and Science Achievement (MESA) Program serves to enrich the experience of students in math, science, and engineering at College of the Canyons. We support financially disadvantaged and/or historically underrepresented students by providing a variety of services, including: academic advisement, discipline-specific enrichment, and community building opportunities. Our primary goal is to equip MESA students with the skills and attitudes necessary for their successful transfer to university.


MESA works closely with industry partners and the University of California, California State University, the California Community Colleges, the Association of Independent Colleges and Universities and the State Department of Education. MESA is nationally recognized for its innovative academic development program.

MESA Center

It is a dedicated space for studying and workshops, with areas for individual and group studying. The MESA Center has nine computers equipped with MatLab, MiniTab, and AutoCad. Students also have access to printers, textbooks, 3-D printers, and a kitchenette.

It is located in Aliso Lab 114 at the Valencia campus.
