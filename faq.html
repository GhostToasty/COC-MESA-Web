FAQs

Requirements

Students must submit an application, be enrolled at College of the Canyons, and have a COC ID card. Once your application is reviewed, you will be notified by email if you meet the requirements.

Fully Qualified MESA Student

  • STEM major (calculus based)
  • Minimum 3 semesters remaining at COC
  • Minimum 2.2 GPA
  • Educationally disadvantaged (first generation college student)
  • Financial need (receiving BOG waiver and/or grants)
  • Plans to transfer to a 4 year college
  • Be a U.S. citizen or resident
  • Either have completed or enrolled in Math 070
    Semester Requirements
    • Attend at least 2 activities/workshops
    • 30+ hours in the center
    • Minimum 2.2 GPA per semester
    • Progress Report
    Yearly Requirements
    • Apply for at least one scholarship, REU, or internship
    • Update your student education plan

Friend of MESA

  • STEM major (calculus based)
  • Minimum 3 semesters remaining at COC
  • Minimum 2.0 GPA
    Semester Requirements
    • Attend at least 1 activity/workshop
    • 20+ hours in the center
    • Minimum 2.0 GPA per semester
  • Biology
  • Bio-Chemistry
  • Chemistry
  • Computer Science
  • Computer Engineering
  • Engineering
  • Geology
  • Math
  • Physics
  • Pre-Dental School
  • Pre-Medical School
  • Pre-Pharmacy School
  • Pre-Veterinary School

Resources

Counselors are avalible online via Zoom or in-person at the MESA Center. Please visit the counselor's page for more information.

If you have a GPA of at least 3.0 and are eligible for financial aid, you may qualify for the Physics/Calculus Book Award Program! You may receive a free textbook if your are enrolled in Physics 220 OR Math 211. A limited number of awards are available so make sure to submit your application as soon as possible! This program is open to all STEM majors. For more information and an application, please visit the MESA Center.
